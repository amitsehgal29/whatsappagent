Our gym offers three membership plans to suit every lifestyle. The Basic plan costs $29/month and includes full access to gym floor equipment, locker rooms, and showers during standard hours. The Premium plan at $59/month adds unlimited group classes, sauna access, and one complimentary personal training session per month. The Family plan at $99/month covers two adults and up to three children under 18, including all Premium benefits plus childcare during workouts.

We operate seven days a week. Monday through Friday we are open from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM. On Saturdays we are open from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Sundays we are open from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary and are posted on our website two weeks in advance. The gym is closed on Christmas Day and New Year's Day.

Our main Downtown location is at 245 Fitness Boulevard, with ample parking in the rear lot and bike racks near the front entrance. The Uptown branch at 890 Wellness Avenue opened in 2024 and features a rooftop yoga deck and an Olympic-size swimming pool. Both locations offer towel service, Wi-Fi, and a smoothie bar in the lobby.

We have two head trainers on staff. Sarah Chen is a certified NASM personal trainer with eight years of experience specialising in strength training, powerlifting, and injury rehabilitation. She holds a degree in Kinesiology and is available for one-on-one sessions Monday through Thursday. Marcus Williams is a Yoga Alliance E-RYT 500 instructor and CrossFit Level 2 trainer who leads our HIIT and mobility classes. He has been teaching for over a decade and also offers nutritional coaching as an add-on service.

Our group class schedule features over thirty classes per week. Yoga is offered Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM with Marcus. HIIT runs Tuesday and Thursday at 6:00 AM, 12:00 PM, and 5:30 PM. Spinning is available Monday through Friday at 7:00 AM and Saturday at 9:00 AM. Zumba is on Tuesday and Thursday evenings at 7:00 PM. Pilates is on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 10:00 AM. All classes are 60 minutes unless noted otherwise.

Amenities at both locations include keycard-secured locker rooms with digital combination locks, rainfall showers with complimentary toiletries, towel service, free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the facility, a smoothie and juice bar, and a recovery zone with foam rollers, massage guns, and compression boots. The Uptown location also has a rooftop yoga deck and a 25-metre pool.

We offer a free trial class for first-time visitors. To book, call or message us with your preferred date and class type. Trial classes are subject to availability and can be booked up to one week in advance. One trial per person; valid photo ID required at check-in.

Our cancellation policy requires four hours' notice for personal training sessions to avoid a $25 late-cancellation fee. Group class reservations can be cancelled up to one hour before start time without penalty. No-shows for two consecutive booked classes result in a one-week booking suspension.

Members can freeze their account once per calendar year for up to three months at no charge. A $10/month holding fee applies for freezes beyond three months. Freeze requests must be submitted in writing at least seven days before the next billing date. Annual memberships paid upfront are not eligible for freezes but may be transferred to another person.

We offer a 15% discount for students, military personnel, first responders, and seniors aged 65 and above. Corporate memberships are available for companies with ten or more employees; contact our membership desk for pricing. A referral bonus of one free month is credited to your account for each new member you refer who signs up for a Premium or Family plan.

Our personal training packages range from single sessions at $75 to a 12-session bundle at $720. Partner training splits the cost between two people at $45 per person per session. Small-group training for three to five people is available at $30 per person per session. All packages include a complimentary fitness assessment and goal-setting consultation.

Parking is free for members for up to two hours at both locations. Extended parking passes are available at the front desk for an additional fee. Bike storage is available indoors at the Downtown location and in a covered rack area at Uptown. Both locations are within a five-minute walk of the nearest metro station.

We have a strict no-solicitation policy on the gym floor. Members are asked to wipe down equipment after use with the provided sanitising spray and towels. Headphones are required for personal audio. Tripods and professional photography equipment are not permitted without prior approval from management.

The pool at Uptown is open during all operating hours. Lap swimming is prioritised from 5:00 AM to 9:00 AM on weekdays. Family swim is on Saturday and Sunday from 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Pool rules require a shower before entry, appropriate swimwear, and children under 14 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

Current promotions include no enrolment fee on Premium and Family plans for the month, a 50% discount on the first month for new members who sign up with a friend, and a free body-composition analysis for anyone who books a gym tour this week.
